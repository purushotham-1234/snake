Constrictor Snakes

Constrictor snakes kill their prey by tightly wrapping around them and cutting off blood flow before swallowing the prey whole.

Common Constrictor Snakes

  • Python
  • Anaconda
  • Boa Constrictor
  • King Snake

Important Facts

  1. Constrictors are non-venomous.
  2. They rely on strength rather than venom.
  3. They often eat large prey.

Constrictor snakes are powerful hunters and an important part of many ecosystems.
