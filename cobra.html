Cobra

The cobra is a venomous snake known for its expandable hood and powerful venom. It is commonly found in Africa and Asia and is one of the most dangerous snakes in the world.

Key Characteristics

  • Venomous snake
  • Expandable hood
  • Fast striking speed
  • Lives in warm regions

Interesting Facts

  1. Cobras raise their hoods to look larger when threatened.
  2. Some cobras can spit venom.
  3. They help control rodent populations.

Cobras are respected in many cultures for their strength and intelligence.
