Python

The python is a large non-venomous snake that kills its prey by constriction. It is found in Africa, Asia, and Australia and is known for its strength and size.

Key Characteristics

  • Non-venomous snake
  • Kills prey by constriction
  • Large and powerful body
  • Lives in forests and grasslands

Interesting Facts

  1. Pythons can swallow prey larger than their head.
  2. They shed their skin several times a year.
  3. Some species lay eggs and protect them.

Pythons are calm but powerful predators and play an important role in nature.
