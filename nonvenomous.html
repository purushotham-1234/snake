Non-Venomous Snakes

Non-venomous snakes do not have venom and usually capture their prey by constriction or swallowing it whole.

Common Non-Venomous Snakes

  • Python
  • Rat Snake
  • King Snake
  • Garter Snake

Important Facts

  1. They are generally harmless to humans.
  2. Bites are rarely dangerous.
  3. They help control pests.

Non-venomous snakes are important for maintaining ecological balance.
