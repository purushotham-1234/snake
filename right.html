Snakes

Snakes are elongated, legless reptiles found in many environments across the world. They are known for their flexible bodies, unique hunting methods, and important role in the ecosystem.

Types of Snakes

  • Venomous Snakes
  • Non-Venomous Snakes
  • Constrictor Snakes
  • Sea Snakes
  • Burrowing Snakes

Common Snake Species

  1. Cobra
  2. Python
  3. Viper
  4. Anaconda

Snakes are crucial for maintaining ecological balance by controlling rodent populations and supporting biodiversity.
