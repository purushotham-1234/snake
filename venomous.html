Venomous Snakes

Venomous snakes use venom to immobilize or kill their prey. Their venom is injected through specialized fangs and can be dangerous to humans.

Common Venomous Snakes

  • Cobra
  • Viper
  • Mamba
  • Rattlesnake

Important Facts

  1. Venom is used mainly for hunting.
  2. Not all venomous snakes are aggressive.
  3. Medical treatment can save lives.

Venomous snakes play an important role in controlling animal populations.
